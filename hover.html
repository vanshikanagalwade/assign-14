In the quiet town of Marbury, the last train pulled into the station. Emma, clutching a worn .
suitcase, hesitated before stepping aboard. The train was nearly empty, save for a few tired.
travelers. She found a seat by the window, watching the familiar landscape blur into the .
night.
Emma was leaving everything behind: the small bakery she’d run for years, the memories of .
a love long gone. As the train chugged forward, a sense of liberation washed over her..
She didn’t know where the tracks would lead, but for the first time in years, she felt the.
thrill of endless possibility..
